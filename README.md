# Rozvoz Specialit Rater

A Chrome browser extension that helps you track and remember your lunch preferences on [RozvozSpecialit.cz](https://www.rozvozspecialit.cz).

## What it does

This extension adds a simple rating system to lunch menus, allowing you to:

- **Rate dishes** with thumbs up, neutral, or thumbs down buttons
- **Remember your preferences** - liked items appear in bold, neutral in orange italics, and disliked items are crossed out
- **Manage your ratings** through a convenient options page
- **Export/import** your rating history
- **Send notifications** via webhook when ratings change (optional)

## Perfect for

- People who regularly order from RozvozSpecialit.cz
- Anyone who wants to track which lunch dishes they've enjoyed (or avoided)
- Teams who want to share lunch ratings via webhook integrations

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repository
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`
3. Enable "Developer mode"
4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the `src` folder
